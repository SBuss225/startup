    Hello and welcome to my startup!

    This is a simple online escape room that I have been building as a part of a Computer Science course at BYU. It is meant to demonstrate my grasp of html, css, javascript, and general web programming concepts.

    For those of you who don't know, an escape room is an activity where one or more players are 'locked' in a room and given some time limit (usually an hour) to complete a series of puzzles in order to escape. This website is meant to simulate this fun puzzle-solving experience virtually.

    Enjoy!
